Audible, New and Improved!

A look into some improvements that could be made to Audible's mobile app.

New

Old

Project Overview

Role: UX Designer & Researcher
Tools: Figma
The Purpose: To expand my portfolio by remaking an app from Audible reviews.

The Problem

According to reviews on the google play store there are numerous design problems. Due to this many have stopped using the service in frustration.

The Goal

To improve the overall user experience by adding more features, deleting useless features and by making the user face more user friendly.

Google Play App Store Reviews Analysis

Strengths

Easy to download audiobooks
The option to archive books from the library
The books are fantastic
Can listen while the screen is off
Listening while offline

Weaknesses

User Interface is confusing
Skipping
Battery Draining
Won't Load audiobooks that are downloaded with poor service
Search Results move when you click on a title and hit the back button
Hard to search for free books
No way to cancel subscription in the app and if there is it's next to impossible to find
No way to easily buy extra credits in the app
Lacks a spot to manage payments
Lacks a cart or add to cart option in the app
No way to easily find archived books
No way to see the price of audiobooks in the app
Doesn't have the option to show chapter and book progress at the same time even though there's room on the page for it
No custom speed presets so you don't have to struggle to hit the right speed
The up and down arrows button doesn't swap first for last and so on
During driving the fast forward 30s is the default instead of rewind 30s as most people use rewind and not fastforward in that situation
No length filter option
There's a more options button which only leads to a wishlist, it could be removed and wishlist could be directly on the first button
No sales page
The horizontal menu is annoying and outdated and the other 3 options are barely used
The discover tab is poitnless as the home page does the same thing and there is a search bar at the top
No option to buy only 1 credit
